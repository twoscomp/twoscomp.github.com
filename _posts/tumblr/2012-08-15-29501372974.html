--- layout: post title: Self Serving Ohio Republicans Officials ---

Republican officials plan on allowing early voting in Republican controlled districts and attempting to deny early/weekend voting in, surprise surprise, Democratically controlled Cincinnati. That seems fair. 

“If you live in Butler or Warren counties in the Republican-leaning suburbs of Cincinnati, you can vote for president beginning in October by going to a polling place in the evening or on weekends…

But, if you live in Cincinnati, you’re out of luck. Republicans on the county election board are planning to end early voting in the city promptly at 5 p.m., and ban it completely on weekends”
