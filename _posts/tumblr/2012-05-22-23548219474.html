I was lucky enough to attend Maker Faire this year thanks to Ben Shih (he won some tickets through Sparkfun and was not able to attend). Maker Faire is just about the coolest thing you can imagine. If you’re not familiar with what it is, think of a science fair x art gallery from elementary school, except all artists and engineers are now adults and haven’t yet learned any limitations.
